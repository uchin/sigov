IGOV Creative Hub

IGOV Creative Hub is a program to increase student capacity in the creative industry with a program focus on graphic design and videography. We are living in a massive digitization, and the demand for the workforce in the creative digital world is increasing. By realizing that not all IGOV students want to work in the government and political sector, the purpose of the IGOV Creative Hub is to create a profile of IGOV graduates who wanted to work in the Digital Creative industry. We help them develop their creativity in the digital world.
